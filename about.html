What is to know

This is an opportunity for you to create and run your own Web based and Digital niche business. You can work from home producing and owning your very own multi income stream website.

Our simple but unique system and technology platform allows you to pull together audio, video and text based content into an online publication and send it out on email to as many people as you wish.

Below are a just a few examples of the revenue streams available from your business; - Advertising space within the content - A host of affiliate products - Your own information products - Google adsense revenue - Automated subscription costs So, for example, if your digital business is called ‘Horses R Us’ and it goes out to 5,000 horse lovers each month, sellers of saddles will want to advertise with you and will pay you for the privilege. They will also visit your website with an array of affiliate products, revenue generating adverts and your own products. No postage cost, no printing cost, no going out in all weathers to stick things through letter boxes! This is an online business you can operate from anywhere in the world so long as you have a computer and the Internet.

This is not a get rich quick scheme! You can earn as much or as little as you desire depending on the amount of hours and hard work you are prepared to put in.

We recommend that you choose a niche that you are passionate about as this will mean you will put much more energy into it. So, if horses are your interest, then ‘Horses R Us’ could be the niche for you to start a business.

In essence, this is your chance to earn a very good living working from home by taking advantage of the fastest growing industry of 2012 don’t take our word for it, below are two examples of highly successful niche businesses.
